### HOW TO INSTALL ###
1) Unpack mod_hoodsSwitchableCloak folder into your Mods folder
2) Copy-paste the content of mod_hoodsSwitchableCloak/SwitchableCloak.input.settings at the top of your Documents\The Witcher 3\input.settings file
4) (Optional) Copy mod_hoodsSwitchableCloak/bin folder to your game root directory
   And then use Menu Filelist Updater (https://www.nexusmods.com/witcher3/mods/7171) or manually add new .xml to your dx11/dx12 txt list

4) That's it.
