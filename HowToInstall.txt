### HOW TO INSTALL? ###
#######################

1) Unpack 📁mod_hoodsSwitchableCloak folder into your 📁Mods folder

2) Copy-paste the content of 📄mod_hoodsSwitchableCloak/SwitchableCloak.input.settings at the top of your 📄Documents\The Witcher 3\input.settings file

3) (Optional) Copy 📁mod_hoodsSwitchableCloak/bin folder to your game root directory
   And then use Menu Filelist Updater (link below) or manually add new .xml to your dx11/dx12 txt list.

4) Use a Script Merger or Script Merger FAE (links below), just to be sure there are no conflicts with other mods

5) That's it.
